    <p><strong>NEW FEATURE: Branch Cite</strong></p>
    <p>A new feature has arrived on Hack Forums, Branch Cite. The idea is to branch the post you cite off to a new thread in the same or new subforum of your choosing. When you begin the branch cite, you'll be given a few subforum suggestions. To better understand the purpose of this feature, think of it like a crosspost on Reddit which allows you to copy a post into a different subreddit or Twitter's threads where you can start a new thread of tweets from within the original tweet.</p>
    <p><a style="font-style: italic; font-size: small;" href="https://hackforums.net/showthread.php?tid=6158061" target="_blank" rel="noopener"> >> Source</a></p>
    <p><strong>Bi-Weekly Editions</strong></p>
    <p>For the next few editions, we will be publishing every other week. We are working on getting more journalists to join the HF News Team so if you are interested, please apply on our <a href="https://hackforums.net/showthread.php?tid=5893696">W4TCHL1ST</a>      invitational thread. We are also making improvements behind the scenes to produce better quality editions. This temporary schedule will give the team the time we need to make all these changes. We will also be posting a thread that links to our blogs so users can easily navigate to our blogs on mobile. Thank you for your understanding.</p>
